Frazzle Dazzle

You are Daisy Dazzle and all of your friends are having bad hair days. Their hair all is all frazzled, so you go out with your trusty blow dryer to find them and make their day fabulous!

This game was made for the Ludum Dare 32 Jam. The Ludum Dare entry page can be found here. All art, music, and code was created by myself. Fonts are used with credit to Eeve Somepx. Check out his stuff here!

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys for movement
  • Z to use blowdryer attack
  • Down while in air to hover
  • While in air, Z + Down to drop attack
